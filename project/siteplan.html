Site Plan for "Explore Guatemala"

Site Name

**Explore Guatemala** - This name was chosen to reflect the content of the website, which highlights and shares information about different places to explore in Guatemala.

Site Purpose

The website will showcase four different places in Guatemala, offering details on their locations, food specialties, and interesting facts. This site aims to share the beauty and culture of Guatemala with a broader audience.

Scenarios

  • What are some popular dishes you can find in specific regions of Guatemala?
  • Where are the best places to visit for a cultural experience in Guatemala?

Color Scheme

Primary Colors:

  • #000357: Used for headings and accents.
  • #D2B48C: Used for background and paragraph text.

Typography

The main font will be **Roboto** for body text and **Merriweather** for headings, giving the site a modern yet professional look.
